# Privacy Policy for Yaque

**Last updated:** 2026-05-14

## Summary

Yaque does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What data is processed?

Yaque stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- Your game history (puzzles solved, solve times, ratings)
- Your settings (board size preferences, etc.)

This data never leaves your device. It is stored in a local database on your phone's internal storage. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

## What data is collected by us?

**None.** Yaque has no internet connection, no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party services. The app does not request the `INTERNET` permission.

## Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with anyone.

## Children's Privacy

Yaque is suitable for all ages. It does not collect data from anyone, including children.

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
